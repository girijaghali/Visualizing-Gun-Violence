In 2018, 6,519 people were killed and 29,678 injured in gun related violence

Embedded deep into the social fabrics of the nation of the United States is the right to bear arms. This constitutional right has been put to the test lately after mass shootings permeated news outlets. How often do they happen?





152 shootings

In the 50 years before the Texas tower shooting, there were just 25 public mass shootings in which four or more people were killed, according to author and criminologist Grant Duwe. Since then, the number has risen dramatically, and many of the deadliest shootings have occurred within the past few years.

SIX KEY POLICIES TO SAVE LIVES FROM GUN VIOLENCE

The grade ranking assigns positive point values to gun safety policies, such as private-sale background checks and extreme risk protection orders, and negative point values to dangerous laws, such as permitless concealed carry. From there, they convert points to letter grades, rank the states, and compare their findings to the latest CDC gun death rates for each state. Every year, the same correlation emerges: high gun law grades are associated with low gun death rates and low gun law grades with high gun death rates.

Goal

We want to visualize gun violence in The United States, put the numbers of gun related violent incidents into perspective, and get a sense of where Americans stand on gun control laws and measures.

Audience

Our audience are members of the general public who have an interest in our subject and are github users, as that is our method for delivery.
